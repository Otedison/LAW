About Harry Brown

Principal Partner at Harry Brown & Associates

Harry Brown is a distinguished advocate with over 15 years of experience practicing law in Nairobi, Kenya. Admitted to the Kenyan Bar in 2008, Harry has built a reputation for excellence in legal representation and client service.

After graduating with honors from the University of Nairobi School of Law, Harry began his career at one of Kenya's leading corporate law firms before establishing his own practice in 2015.
